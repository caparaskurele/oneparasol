Contract essentials
Check representations, warranties, indemnities, escrow and closing conditions. Understand limitation of liability and survival periods.

Governance and minority protections
Look for tag/drag rights, pre-emptive rights and information covenants.

When to use counsel
Use qualified counsel for structuring, cross-border contracts and regulatory/licensing issues.

Conclusion
Basic legal literacy helps investors spot red flags and structure protections proportionate to risk.
